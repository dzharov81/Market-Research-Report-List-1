<p><ul><li>Immersion Heaters</li><li>Radiator Heaters</li><li>Pad Style Heaters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive auxiliary PTC heaters for electric vehicles are heating devices used to warm up the vehicle's cabin during cold weather. Immersion heaters are installed directly into the vehicle's coolant system and heat it up, transferring warmth to the cabin through the vehicle's heating system. Radiator heaters are mounted on the vehicle's radiator and use the engine's coolant fluid to generate heat. Pad-style heaters are placed on the vehicle's seats or floor mats, directly heating the surface. These different types provide efficient heating solutions for electric vehicles, ensuring passenger comfort in chilly conditions.</p></p>
