<p><p>The linen bed sheets market consists of two primary types of fabrics: pure linen fabrics and linen blended fabrics. Pure linen fabrics are made entirely from flax fibers, which are known for their strength, durability, and high-quality texture. These sheets are highly breathable, hypoallergenic, and have moisture-wicking properties, making them ideal for hot sleepers or those with allergies. Linen blended fabrics, on the other hand, are made by combining flax fibers with other materials like cotton or polyester. These sheets offer a combination of the benefits of linen and other fabrics, such as increased softness and wrinkle resistance.</p></p>
