<p><p>The Acoustic Fiber Glass market includes three types: Continuous Fiber, Fixed Length Fiber, and Glass Wool. Continuous Fiber refers to fibrous material made of glass that is continuously produced, offering high strength and insulation properties. Fixed Length Fiber refers to glass fibers that are cut into specific lengths, allowing flexibility in applications. Glass Wool is a type of fibrous material made of glass, formed into insulation products like rolls or boards, providing excellent thermal and sound insulation properties. These three types cater to various needs in the acoustic insulation industry.</p></p>
