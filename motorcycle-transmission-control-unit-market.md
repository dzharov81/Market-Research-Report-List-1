TCUs play a crucial role in optimizing the performance and efficiency of electric and hybrid motorcycles by managing the power delivery and motor control systems.</p><p>Moreover, advancements in technology, including the integration of artificial intelligence and machine learning capabilities in TCUs, are anticipated to provide growth opportunities for market players.
