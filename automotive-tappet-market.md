<p><strong>The Automotive Tappet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flat Tappet</li><li>Roller Tappet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive tappet market encompasses two main types: flat tappets and roller tappets. Flat tappets are engine components that slide back and forth in contact with the camshaft to open and close the valves. They are cost-effective but require regular maintenance and precise lubrication to prevent wear. On the other hand, roller tappets are equipped with rollers on their surfaces, reducing friction and wear. They are more durable and reliable, ideal for high-performance engines. Both types offer their own advantages, catering to different engine needs and preferences in the automotive market.</p></p>
