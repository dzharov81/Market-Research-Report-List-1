<p><strong>The Material Handling Automatic Robotics Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Articulated Material Handling Robot</li><li>SCARA Material Handling Robot</li><li>Parallel Material Handling Robot</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Material Handling Automatic Robotics Machine Market can be classified into three types based on their structure and movement capabilities. Firstly, the Articulated Material Handling Robot consists of multiple linked segments, offering a wide range of motion and flexibility. Secondly, the SCARA Material Handling Robot possesses a vertical joint structure that limits its movement to a two-dimensional plane, making it suitable for assembly line tasks. Lastly, the Parallel Material Handling Robot comprises multiple arms operating independently or in tandem, enabling higher payload capabilities and increased productivity.</p></p>
