<p><p>Silicon oxide nanomaterials are classified into different market types based on their purity levels. The types include 2N, 3N, 4N, and other. 2N denotes a purity level of 99%, meaning that 1 out of every 100 atoms is impure. Similarly, 3N represents a purity level of 99.9% with 1 impure atom out of every 1000. The 4N market type refers to a purity level of 99.99%, indicating only 1 impure atom out of every 10,000. The "other" market type encompasses all purity levels that do not fall into the aforementioned categories.</p></p>
