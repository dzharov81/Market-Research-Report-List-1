<p><strong>The Automotive Telematics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plug and Play Telematics</li><li>Hardwired Install Telematics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plug and Play Telematics refer to a type of automotive telematics solution that can be easily plugged into the vehicle's OBD-II port. This technology allows for real-time data collection and analysis, providing information on vehicle diagnostics, maintenance reminders, and driver behavior. On the other hand, Hardwired Install Telematics involves permanently installing telematics hardware within the vehicle's electrical system. This solution offers more advanced features such as GPS tracking, remote vehicle control, and comprehensive data analytics for fleet management applications.</p></p>
