<p><strong>The Beer Dispense Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Draw System</li><li>Air Cooled System</li><li>Glycol Cooled System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Beer Dispense Systems Market Types consist of Direct Draw System, Air Cooled System, and Glycol Cooled System. Direct Draw Systems are compact and ideal for small establishments, where the beer is directly refrigerated and served from the keg to the tap, requiring minimal cooling. Air Cooled Systems are suitable for medium-sized establishments, as they cool the beer by blowing cold air from the refrigeration unit into the beer tower. Glycol Cooled Systems are used in larger establishments, cooling beer through a separate system of insulated lines carrying chilled glycol, ensuring precise temperature control and longer beer lines.</p></p>
