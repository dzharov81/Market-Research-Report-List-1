<p><p>The eVTOL (Electric Vertical Takeoff and Landing) jet market consists of two main types: All Electric and Hybrid. All Electric eVTOLs are powered solely by electricity, utilizing batteries or fuel cells for propulsion. They offer zero-emission flying and quieter operation. On the other hand, Hybrid eVTOLs combine both electric propulsion and traditional engines, allowing for longer flight ranges and increased payload capacity. Hybrid models can take advantage of electric power during takeoff and landing, while using conventional engines for cruising, providing a balance between efficiency and performance.</p></p>
