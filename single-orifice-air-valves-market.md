<p><p>The single orifice air valves market can be categorized into two types: flanged and threaded. Flanged air valves have a connection with bolt holes and a flat face, allowing for easy installation and removal. They provide a secure and leak-proof connection. On the other hand, threaded air valves have a threaded connection, which requires screwing onto the pipe. They are suitable for systems with limited space and offer a reliable and tight connection. Both options fulfill the same purpose of regulating air flow but differ in terms of their connection method.</p></p>
