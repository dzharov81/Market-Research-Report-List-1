<p><p>In the 5G market, PCB (Printed Circuit Board) comes in different types such as Single Panel, Double Panel, and Multilayer Panel. Single Panel PCB consists of a single layer of substrate, while Double Panel PCB has two layers of substrate with tracks on both sides. Multilayer Panel PCB comprises multiple layers of substrate stacked together, allowing for more complex circuit designs. These different PCB types cater to various requirements in the 5G market, offering greater flexibility, reliability, and functionality to support the advanced technologies of 5G networks.</p></p>
