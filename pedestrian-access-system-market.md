Swing gates are barriers that open and close, allowing one person to pass through at a time. Turnstiles are rotating gates that only allow one person to pass through with each rotation. Others refer to different types of access control systems, such as sliding gates or optical barriers. These systems provide efficient and secure pedestrian access in various settings, including airports, stadiums, and office buildings.</p></p>
