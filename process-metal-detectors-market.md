<p><p>The market for process metal detectors can be categorized into various types. Belt process metal detectors are used in conveyor systems to detect any metallic contaminants in the products being transported. Stationary process metal detectors are installed in fixed positions within a production line to identify any metal impurities. Handheld process metal detectors are portable devices that allow for manual scanning and identification of metallic objects. Other types of process metal detectors include gravity-feed systems and pipeline metal detectors, which are specifically designed for their respective applications.</p></p>
