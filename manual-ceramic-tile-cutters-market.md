<p><p>The manual ceramic tile cutters market offers two main types: single track and double track cutters. Single track manual tile cutters are designed with a single guide rail, allowing for precise and straight cuts on ceramic tiles. On the other hand, double track manual tile cutters feature dual guide rails, enabling users to cut larger tiles or perform angled cuts with ease. Both types are widely used in the construction industry, offering efficient and accurate tile cutting solutions for various projects.</p></p>
