<p><p>A Speed Limitation Device (SLD) is an electronic system used to restrict the maximum speed of vehicles. There are different types of SLDs available in the market. Fuel Type Speed Limiters regulate the engine's fuel supply, thereby limiting the vehicle's speed. Electronic Pedal Interface Speed Limiters modify the electronic signal between the accelerator pedal and the engine, controlling the vehicle's speed. Both types are designed to enhance safety and fuel efficiency by preventing vehicles from exceeding a preset speed limit.</p></p>
