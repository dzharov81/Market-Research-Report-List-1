<p><p>Graphene paper market comprises two main types: graphene oxide paper and reduced graphene oxide paper. Graphene oxide paper is produced by oxidizing graphite, resulting in a sheet-like material with oxygen functional groups. It offers good mechanical strength, high electrical conductivity, and thermal stability. On the other hand, reduced graphene oxide paper is obtained by selectively removing the oxygen functional groups from graphene oxide paper, enhancing its mechanical properties and electrical conductivity. Both types have potential applications in various industries, such as electronics, energy storage, and composites.</p></p>
