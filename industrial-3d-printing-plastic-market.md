<p><p>The industrial 3D printing plastic market can be categorized into three types: powder, filament, and liquid. In the powder market, plastics are pulverized into fine particles which are then melted and solidified layer by layer to create 3D printed objects. In the filament market, plastics are extruded into a filament form which is then heated and added layer by layer to form the desired object. In the liquid market, a liquid resin is exposed to UV light, selectively hardening it to form the intricate shapes of the 3D printed object.</p></p>
