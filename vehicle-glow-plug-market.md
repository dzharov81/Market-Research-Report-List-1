<p><p>The vehicle glow plug market consists of two main types: metal glow plugs and ceramic glow plugs. Metal glow plugs are typically made of steel or other metal alloys and provide a reliable and efficient heating solution for diesel engines. They are known for their durability, conductivity, and ability to withstand high temperatures. On the other hand, ceramic glow plugs are made of ceramic materials and offer enhanced performance in terms of heat retention and fast warm-up time. These plugs are lighter in weight and more resistant to corrosion, ensuring optimal engine efficiency.</p></p>
