<p><p>The vehicle transfer case market comprises three main types: Four Wheel drive (4WD), All-Wheel drive (AWD), and Others. Four Wheel drive (4WD) systems distribute power evenly to all four wheels, enhancing traction in off-road conditions. All-Wheel drive (AWD) systems distribute power to all four wheels but can vary the amount of power sent to each wheel, improving performance on various terrains. The Others category includes transfer cases with specific features like electronic control or torque vectoring systems. These different types cater to diverse customer preferences and specific driving needs.</p></p>
