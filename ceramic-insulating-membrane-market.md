Plate Type Ceramic Membrane refers to a flat structure, while Tubular Type Ceramic Membrane consists of cylindrical tubes. Multichannel Ceramic Membrane, on the other hand, comprises multiple channels for fluid flow. These membrane types are used in various industries for insulation purposes, providing resistance to high temperatures and chemicals while allowing selective permeation of gases or liquids.</p></p>
