<p><p>The Automotive Metal and Plastic Fasteners Market can be classified into various types such as bolts, nuts, rivets, screws, washers, and others. Bolts are threaded cylindrical fasteners used to hold two or more components together. Nuts are hexagonal or square-shaped fasteners that secure bolts in place. Rivets are permanent fasteners used to join two or more pieces of material together. Screws are threaded fasteners that provide a tighter grip. Washers are flat discs used to distribute the load and prevent damage. Other fasteners include pins, clips, and plugs used for specific applications.</p></p>
