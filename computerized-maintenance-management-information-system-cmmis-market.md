<p><p>A computerized maintenance management information system (CMMIS) is a software application used to manage maintenance operations in organizations. The market for CMMIS can be categorized into two types: Cloud Based and On-Premises. Cloud-based systems are hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access and manage data from any location with an internet connection. On-premises systems are installed and operated on local servers, requiring physical infrastructure and maintenance. Both types offer functionalities for organizing work orders, tracking assets, scheduling maintenance, and generating reports, but differ in terms of accessibility, cost, and control.</p></p>
