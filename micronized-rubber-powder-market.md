<p><p>Micronized rubber powder (MRP) is a high-performance additive derived from scrap tires that have undergone a mechanical grinding process to reduce the rubber particles to a fine powder with a particle size range of less than 500 microns. MRP possesses unique characteristics, such as improved compatibility with various polymers, increased surface area, and enhanced performance capabilities. It finds wide-ranging applications, including in the manufacturing of tires, construction materials, adhesives, sealants, and coatings.</p><p>The future outlook for the micronized rubber powder market appears highly promising.
