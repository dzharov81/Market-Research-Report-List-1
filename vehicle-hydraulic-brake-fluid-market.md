<p><strong>The Vehicle Hydraulic Brake Fluid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DOT3</li><li>DOT4</li><li>DOT5</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vehicle hydraulic brake fluid market consists of various types, namely DOT3, DOT4, DOT5, and others. DOT3 brake fluid is a glycol-based fluid suitable for most vehicles, providing a lower boiling point compared to DOT4. DOT4 brake fluid is also glycol-based but offers a higher boiling point, making it more suitable for high-performance vehicles. DOT5, on the other hand, is a silicone-based fluid with a different composition, providing benefits like longer lifespan and resistance to moisture. The "Others" category includes alternative brake fluids like mineral oil or specialty fluids specific to certain vehicle models or applications.</p></p>
