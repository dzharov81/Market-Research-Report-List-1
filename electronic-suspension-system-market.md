<p><strong>The Electronic Suspension System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Semi-Active Suspension System</li><li>Active Suspension System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electronic Suspension System market consists of two major types: Semi-Active Suspension System and Active Suspension System. </p><p>Semi-Active Suspension System utilizes an electronic control unit to continuously adjust the suspension characteristics based on inputs such as road conditions and driving dynamics. It offers improved ride comfort and stability while maintaining a good balance between comfort and handling.</p><p>Active Suspension System, on the other hand, employs electronic sensors and actuators to actively control each wheel's suspension independently. This allows for real-time adjustments to optimize ride quality, handling, and stability, particularly in high-performance vehicles.</p></p>
