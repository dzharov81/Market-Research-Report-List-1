The market types for SiC Fiber include Preceramic Polymer Pyrolysis and Chemical Vapor Reaction processes. In the Preceramic Polymer Pyrolysis method, a preceramic polymer is subjected to high temperatures, resulting in the formation of SiC Fiber. On the other hand, the Chemical Vapor Reaction process involves the reaction of carbon-containing gas with silicon-containing gas at high temperatures to produce SiC Fiber. These market types offer different approaches to manufacturing SiC Fiber.</p></p>
