Injection/extrusion grade PVDF is commonly used in the manufacturing of pipes, profiles, films, and sheets, as it has good melt flow and processability. Adhesive/film grade PVDF is often employed in the production of adhesive tapes, films, and laminates, as it exhibits excellent adhesion properties. Other grade PVDF encompasses specialized applications such as PVDF-based membranes for water treatment and medical devices.</p></p>
