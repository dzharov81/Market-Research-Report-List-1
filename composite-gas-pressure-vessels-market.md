<p><p>Composite gas pressure vessels are widely used for storing and transporting compressed gases. The market includes different types such as Type III and Type IV vessels. Type III vessels consist of a metal liner wrapped with a composite material, providing good impact resistance and mechanical strength. Type IV vessels have a fully composite structure, utilizing high-strength carbon fibers for enhanced lightweight properties. Both types offer advantages such as improved safety, reduced weight, and increased storage capacity. These vessels cater to various industries including automotive, aerospace, and alternative fuel storage.</p></p>
