<p><strong>The Embedded Vibration Monitoring System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Accelerometers</li><li>Proximity probes</li><li>Velocity sensors</li><li>Transmitters</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The embedded vibration monitoring system market encompasses various types of devices used for monitoring vibrations in machines and structures. Accelerometers are commonly used to measure the acceleration of an object and convert it into an electrical signal. Proximity probes are utilized to monitor the distance between the probe and a target, determining the vibration level. Velocity sensors measure the rate of change of displacement. Transmitters facilitate the transmission of vibration data to a central monitoring system. Other types of devices may include temperature sensors, pressure sensors, or strain gauges used in conjunction with vibration monitoring systems.</p></p>
