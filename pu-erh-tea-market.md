<p><p>Pu-erh tea, a type of fermented tea, is divided into two market types: raw and cooked. Raw Pu-erh, also known as Sheng Pu-erh, is made from fresh tea leaves and undergoes a natural aging process. It has a vibrant flavor profile and can improve over time. Cooked Pu-erh, also called Shou Pu-erh, undergoes an accelerated fermentation process, resulting in a dark infusion. This type has a mellow and earthy taste. Both types are popular among tea enthusiasts and offer unique characteristics that develop with aging.</p></p>
