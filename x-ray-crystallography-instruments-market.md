<p><p>The X-Ray Crystallography Instruments Market consists of two main types: X-Ray Powder Diffraction and Single-crystal XRD. X-Ray Powder Diffraction is a technique used to determine the atomic and molecular structure of a material by analyzing the diffraction pattern produced when x-rays are passed through a powdered sample. This method is widely used in various fields, such as material science and pharmaceuticals. On the other hand, Single-crystal XRD is used to analyze the atomic arrangement of a single crystal sample, providing detailed information about the crystal structure and its properties. This method is commonly used in the study of minerals, inorganic compounds, and organic compounds.</p></p>
