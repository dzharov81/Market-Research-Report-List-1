<p><p>A nonstick cooker is a kitchen appliance that is designed to provide a non-stick surface for cooking various types of food. These cookers require less oil or fat to cook food, making it a healthier cooking option. Additionally, the non-stick coating prevents food from burning, resulting in a healthier and tastier meal.</p><p>Furthermore, the increasing number of households, especially in urban areas, is contributing to the growth of the nonstick cooker market.
