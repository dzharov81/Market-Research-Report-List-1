<p><p>Electric-vehicle batteries, or EV batteries, come in several different types. The NCM/NCA market consists of nickel-cobalt-manganese or nickel-cobalt-aluminum batteries, which offer high energy density and are commonly used in electric vehicles. The LFP market comprises lithium-iron-phosphate batteries, known for their long cycle life and enhanced safety. LCO batteries are lithium-cobalt-oxide batteries, which have high energy density but are less stable. LMO batteries are lithium-manganese-oxide batteries, providing moderate energy density. Additionally, there are other types of EV batteries available in the market, each with their own unique characteristics and applications.</p></p>
