<p><p>Purging compounds are used in various industries to clean and remove residue from machinery and equipment. There are three main types of purging compounds: mechanical, chemical/foaming, and liquid. Mechanical purging compounds involve the use of abrasive materials to scrub away contaminants. Chemical/foaming purging compounds utilize foaming agents and chemicals to dissolve and remove residues. Liquid purging compounds are non-abrasive solutions that are used to flush out and clean machinery. Each type of purging compound has its own advantages and is suitable for specific applications.</p></p>
