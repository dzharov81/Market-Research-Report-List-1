<p><p>MRI machines are used for diagnostic imaging purposes to obtain detailed images of the internal structures of the body. Permanent magnet MRI machines use a fixed magnetic field generated by permanent magnets, making them simpler in design and less expensive, but have lower magnetic field strength. Superconducting magnet MRI machines use superconducting coils cooled with liquid helium to generate a higher magnetic field strength, allowing for better image quality. These machines are more complex and expensive due to their cooling requirements.</p></p>
