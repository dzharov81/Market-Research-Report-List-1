<p><p>High Definition Oscilloscopes are classified into two types: Hand-held Oscillograph and Table-type Oscilloscopes. Hand-held Oscillographs are portable oscilloscopes that can be easily carried and used in various applications. They are compact, battery-powered devices with a built-in display, ideal for on-the-go measurements. On the other hand, Table-type Oscilloscopes are larger stationary devices that provide more advanced features and functionalities. They typically have larger displays, better signal processing capabilities, and are suitable for more complex measurements in laboratory or industrial settings.</p></p>
