<p><p>Electroplating post-treatment agents are used to enhance the properties of electroplated metals. The market is categorized into three types based on their chemical nature: acidic, neutral, and alkalinity. Acidic agents are commonly used to remove impurities, oxide layers, and tarnish from metal surfaces. Neutral agents serve as a protective layer, preventing corrosion and improving the metal's appearance. Alkalinity agents, on the other hand, aid in improving adhesion and interaction between the plated metal and the surrounding environment. These different types cater to diverse electroplating applications and provide specific benefits to the treated metals.</p></p>
