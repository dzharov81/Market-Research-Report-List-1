<p><p>CPE (Chlorinated Polyethylene) Rubber Compound is a type of synthetic rubber compound that is widely used in various industries due to its excellent properties such as weather resistance, heat resistance, and chemical resistance. There are two main types of CPE rubber compounds in the market: HCPE (High Chlorinated Polyethylene) and CPE. HCPE rubber compound is highly chlorinated and offers higher hardness and tensile strength, making it ideal for applications requiring increased durability and resistance. On the other hand, CPE rubber compound has lower chlorine content and is more flexible, making it suitable for applications that require good flexibility and impact resistance.</p></p>
