<p><p>The liquid desiccant dehumidifier market can be categorized into two types based on the solvent used: organic solvent and inorganic solvent. Organic solvent-based dehumidifiers use liquid desiccants such as ethylene glycol or propylene glycol, which have low toxicity and are biodegradable. These dehumidifiers are commonly used in residential and commercial applications. On the other hand, inorganic solvent-based dehumidifiers use liquid desiccants like lithium chloride or calcium chloride, which have high absorbent capacities. These dehumidifiers are commonly used in industrial applications where high humidity levels need to be controlled efficiently.</p></p>
