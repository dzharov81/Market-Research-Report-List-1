The market for these films can be classified into three types based on their composition: PET-based, PS-based, and others. PET-based diffuser films are made from polyethylene terephthalate, which offers excellent clarity and impact resistance. PS-based diffuser films are made from polystyrene, known for its good light diffusion properties. The "others" category includes diffuser films made from different materials such as acrylic or polycarbonate, which provide specific optical characteristics for diverse applications.</p></p>
