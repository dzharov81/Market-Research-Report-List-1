<p><p>The epoxy mortar market consists of two main types: two-component epoxy mortar and three-component epoxy mortar. Two-component epoxy mortar is a mixture of resin and hardener that requires a specific mixing ratio for proper curing. It is commonly used for applications such as concrete repair and flooring due to its high strength and durability. On the other hand, three-component epoxy mortar consists of resin, hardener, and aggregates. It is mainly used for heavy-duty applications that require exceptional resistance to chemicals, impact, and thermal stress.</p></p>
