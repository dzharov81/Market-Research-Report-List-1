<p><p>ASA stands for Automated Spread Assembly, which is a type of derivatives market that allows traders to electronically assemble and execute spread trades. ODSA (Open Discriminatory Spread Assembly) is similar to OSA, but it enables traders to view and trade discriminatory spread instruments publicly. NSA (Negotiated Spread Assembly) is a market type where traders negotiate spread terms privately and execute trades electronically. Lastly, DDSA (Discriminatory and Negotiated Spread Assembly) allows traders to both negotiate and view discriminatory spread instruments privately or publicly.</p></p>
