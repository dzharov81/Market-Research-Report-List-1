These include blood tubing sets, arterial tubing sets, and venous tubing sets. Blood tubing sets are commonly used to connect the patient's blood vessels to the dialyzer machine for the removal of waste products from the blood. Arterial tubing sets carry the blood from the patient's body to the dialyzer, while venous tubing sets return the cleaned blood back to the patient.</p></p>
