<p><p>A reach forklift, also known as a narrow aisle forklift, is a type of industrial truck that is specifically designed for narrow spaces. The market analysis suggests that the growth in e-commerce and logistics industries are the primary drivers for the reach forklift market. Reach forklifts are designed to maneuver in tight spaces, allowing businesses to maximize their storage capacity.
