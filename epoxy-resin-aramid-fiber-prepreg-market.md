<p><ul><li>Thermoset</li><li>Thermo-plastic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The epoxy resin aramid fiber prepreg market consists of two main types: thermoset and thermoplastic. Thermoset prepregs are made from a combination of epoxy resin and aramid fiber, which are chemically cross-linked during curing, resulting in a rigid and durable material. On the other hand, thermoplastic prepregs are formed using epoxy resin and aramid fiber that can be repeatedly melted and solidified without changing their chemical structure. This allows for easier processing and recycling. Both types offer different advantages and are used in various applications within industries such as aerospace, automotive, and construction.</p></p>
