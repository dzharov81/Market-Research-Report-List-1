<p><p>α-Pyrrolidone, also known as 2-Pyrrolidone or α-P, is a non-toxic, water-soluble cyclic amide. It is also used as a precursor in the synthesis of various drugs and pharmaceutical intermediates. In the agrochemical industry, α-Pyrrolidone is used as a solvent for formulating pesticides. It also finds application as a solvent for dyes, resins, and polymers in the electronics industry. In the cosmetic industry, it is used as an ingredient in skincare products for its moisturizing and emollient properties.</p><p>The future outlook for the α-Pyrrolidone market is expected to be positive and promising.
