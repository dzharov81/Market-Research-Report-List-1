<p><p>The medical angiography devices market includes three main types: X-ray angiography devices, CT angiography devices, and MR angiography devices. X-ray angiography devices use X-rays to create detailed images of blood vessels, allowing doctors to diagnose and treat conditions affecting the arteries and veins. CT angiography devices use computed tomography technology to generate 3D images of blood vessels and provide information about blood flow. MR angiography devices use magnetic resonance imaging to visualize blood vessels and detect any abnormalities. These devices play a crucial role in diagnosing and guiding treatment for various cardiovascular conditions.</p></p>
