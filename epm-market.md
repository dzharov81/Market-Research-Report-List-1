<p><p>EPM (Ethylene Propylene Monomer) is a type of synthetic rubber used in various industries. Solution Polymerization is a market type where the polymerization process occurs in a liquid solvent, resulting in higher purity and consistency of the EPM product. Suspension Polymerization, on the other hand, involves the polymerization process taking place in a suspension medium, resulting in particles of EPM dispersed in a liquid. This method is useful for producing EPM with different particle sizes and properties. Both market types cater to specific industrial needs and applications of EPM.</p></p>
