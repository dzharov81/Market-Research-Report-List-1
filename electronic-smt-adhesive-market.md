<p><ul><li>One Component</li><li>Multicomponent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic SMT adhesive market is categorized into two types: one-component and multicomponent. One-component adhesives are pre-mixed adhesives that only require heat or ultraviolet (UV) light to cure. They are easy to handle, store, and apply, making them suitable for small-scale applications. On the other hand, multicomponent adhesives consist of two or more components that need to be mixed together before application. They offer better performance and flexibility, but require accurate mixing and dispensing systems, making them more suitable for large-scale industrial applications.</p></p>
