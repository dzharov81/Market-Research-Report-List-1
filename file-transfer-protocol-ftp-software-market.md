<p><p>The FTP Software Market can be divided into two types: Cloud-Based and On-Premises. Cloud-Based FTP software operates on remote servers and allows users to access and transfer files through the internet. It offers flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness as it eliminates the need for hardware infrastructure. On the other hand, On-Premises FTP software is installed on local servers and requires physical management.
