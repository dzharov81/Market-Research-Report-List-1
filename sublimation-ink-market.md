<p><p>Sublimation ink is categorized into two types: water-based dye sublimation ink and solvent dye sublimation ink. Water-based dye sublimation ink is made by dissolving dyes in water, which makes it more environmentally friendly and suitable for printing on various substrates like textiles and ceramics. On the other hand, solvent dye sublimation ink is formulated with organic solvents, making it more versatile for printing on materials like vinyl, plastic, and metal. Both types of sublimation ink offer vibrant and long-lasting colors when used in sublimation printing processes.</p></p>
