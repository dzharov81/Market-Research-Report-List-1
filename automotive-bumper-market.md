<p><p>The automotive bumper market consists of two main types: front bumpers and rear bumpers. Front bumpers are located at the front of the vehicle and serve to absorb impact during collisions, protecting the engine and other crucial components. Rear bumpers, on the other hand, are positioned at the back of the vehicle and offer protection to the trunk, taillights, and other rear-end components. Both front and rear bumpers are designed to enhance safety and minimize damage in the event of a crash.</p></p>
