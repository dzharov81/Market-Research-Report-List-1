<p><p>Natural Hydraulic Lime (NHL) is a type of lime that is derived from limestone containing natural hydraulic properties. It is produced by burning limestone with a high clay content at a lower temperature than that used for producing quicklime. The resulting lime has hydraulic properties, meaning it can set and harden when exposed to water, making it suitable for construction applications.</p><p>NHL is widely used as a binder in historical building restoration and conservation projects, as it provides a breathable, flexible, and durable material that is compatible with traditional building techniques. It also offers advantages such as self-healing properties, resistance to water damage, and low carbon footprint, making it an environmentally friendly alternative to other construction materials.</p><p>The Natural Hydraulic Lime (NHL) market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years.
