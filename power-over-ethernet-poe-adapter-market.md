<p><p>The Power Over Ethernet (PoE) Adapter market can be categorized into two types: Client Side and Server Market. The Client Side PoE Adapter refers to the devices that receive power and data through an Ethernet cable from a PoE switch or injector. They are commonly used in IP cameras, wireless access points, and VoIP phones. On the other hand, the Server Market PoE Adapter includes devices that are responsible for delivering power and data to the client-side devices, such as PoE switches and injectors. These adapters enable the transmission of both power and data over a single Ethernet cable, simplifying installation and reducing costs.</p></p>
