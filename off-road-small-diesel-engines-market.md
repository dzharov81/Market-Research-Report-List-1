<p><p>The off-road small diesel engines market can be categorized based on their cooling systems. In contrast, water-cooled engines use a liquid coolant system that circulates around the engine to maintain an optimal operating temperature. This type is more common in larger off-road vehicles. Air-cooled engines utilize a cooling fan and fins to direct air over the engine, offering simplicity and reliability for smaller off-road applications.</p></p>
