<p><p>Spunmelt nonwoven, also known as spunbond-meltblown-spunbond (SMS) nonwoven, is a type of fabric made by combining the spunbond and meltblown processes. These filaments are then bonded together using heat or chemicals to form a strong, durable, and highly versatile fabric.</p><p>The spunmelt nonwoven market has witnessed significant growth in recent years due to its increasing adoption in various industries such as healthcare, automotive, agriculture, personal care, and construction.
