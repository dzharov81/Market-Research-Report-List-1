<p><p>The scooter tire market consists of two main types: solid tire and pneumatic tire. Solid tires are made of rubber and do not require air inflation, providing a maintenance-free option. They are ideal for heavy-duty use as they are puncture-proof and offer better stability. On the other hand, pneumatic tires are air-filled and provide a cushioning effect, offering a smoother ride. They are suitable for various terrains but require regular maintenance and are more vulnerable to punctures. Both types cater to different scooter users' preferences and usage requirements.</p></p>
