<p><p>The Electronic Differential Lock (EDL) is a technology used in automobiles to improve traction and stability by controlling the distribution of torque between the wheels. It electronically monitors each wheel's speed and when it detects a wheel losing traction, it applies braking force to that wheel to redirect power to the wheel with better traction. This enhances the vehicle's handling and stability, especially in challenging driving conditions such as slippery roads or off-road terrains.</p><p>The future outlook of the Electronic Differential Lock Market appears promising. The rising demand for vehicle safety and stability systems, along with the increasing adoption of electronic control systems in vehicles, are expected to drive the market growth.
