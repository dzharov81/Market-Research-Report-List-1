<p><p>Commercial and industrial microgrids refer to self-sustained energy systems that cater to the power needs of businesses and industries. AC microgrid systems use alternating current to distribute power, making them compatible with existing electrical infrastructure. DC microgrid systems, on the other hand, utilize direct current for power distribution and are more suitable for applications like data centers. Hybrid microgrid systems combine both AC and DC technologies to offer flexible and efficient power solutions. Each market type offers unique advantages and caters to specific requirements in the commercial and industrial sectors.</p></p>
