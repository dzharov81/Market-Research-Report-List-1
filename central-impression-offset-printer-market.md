<p><p>Central Impression Offset Printer Market is categorized into two types: Automatic and Semi-automatic. It requires minimal human intervention and ensures high productivity and accurate printing results. Conversely, a Semi-automatic Central Impression Offset Printer requires some manual operations, including plate loading and ink adjustments.
