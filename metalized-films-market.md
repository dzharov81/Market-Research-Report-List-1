<p><p>Metalized films are a type of packaging material that has a thin layer of metal, typically aluminum, applied to one side. There are different types of metalized films available in the market. Gloss voided metalized films have a glossy appearance and are created by vacuum coating aluminum on the film. Reflective metalized films have a high reflective property and are used for various applications. Transparent metalized films have a transparent appearance and provide an attractive packaging solution. Voided metalized films have small holes in the metal layer, allowing for superior moisture and gas barrier properties.</p></p>
