<p><p>In the market for highway guard rails, there are various types available. </p><p>1. Cable Type: These guard rails consist of steel cables tensioned between posts, providing a flexible barrier that redirects vehicles upon impact.</p><p>2. W-Beam Type: This type of guard rail has a w-shaped profile and is commonly used due to its ability to absorb and deflect the impact forces from vehicles, minimizing the damage caused.</p><p>3. Thrie-Beam Type: Thrie-beam guard rails are similar to w-beam type but with a larger profile, providing enhanced protection and reducing the risk of vehicle under-riding.</p><p>4. Others: This category includes different types of guard rails such as concrete barriers, steel barriers, and bollards, each designed for specific applications and scenarios.</p></p>
