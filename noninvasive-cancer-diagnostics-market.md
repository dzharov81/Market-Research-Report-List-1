<p><p>Noninvasive Cancer Diagnostics refers to methods of diagnosing cancer without the need for invasive procedures such as biopsies. These tests are less invasive and provide real-time information about the genetic makeup of the tumor, helping in early detection, monitoring treatment response, and identifying drug resistance. Liquid biopsy tests also allow for repeated sampling, providing a dynamic view of the tumor's genetic changes over time.</p><p>Another trend is the integration of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning algorithms in cancer diagnostics.
