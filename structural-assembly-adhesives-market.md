<p><p>Structural assembly adhesives are used in various industries for bonding and joining applications. Two common types of these adhesives are paste and tape. Paste adhesives are a thick consistency and are typically applied manually using a spatula or a dispenser. They offer good adhesive strength and are commonly used for bonding applications that require gap filling. On the other hand, tape adhesives consist of a thin layer of adhesive coated on a flexible backing material. They are easy to apply, provide high tack, and are commonly used in industries where speed and convenience are important.</p></p>
