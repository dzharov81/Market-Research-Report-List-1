<p><p>The welded steel tube market comprises two main types: straight seam welded pipe and spiral welded pipe. Straight seam welded pipe is manufactured by forming a single continuous seam along its length, making it suitable for various applications. On the other hand, spiral welded pipe is produced by helically welding steel coils to create a larger diameter pipe. It is commonly used in industries that require a high strength-to-weight ratio and resistance to corrosion.</p></p>
