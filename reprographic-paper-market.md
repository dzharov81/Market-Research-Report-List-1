<p><strong>The Reprographic Paper Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Below 30 gsm</li><li>30-90 gsm</li><li>91-120 gsm</li><li>121-180 gsm</li><li>More than 180 gsm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Reprographic paper market types are categorized by their weight, measured in grams per square meter (gsm). Papers below 30 gsm are ultra-lightweight, suitable for delicate tasks like tracing or drafting. The 30-90 gsm range is commonly used for everyday printing needs. Papers between 91-120 gsm offer increased thickness and durability, suitable for brochures or flyers. The 121-180 gsm range provides heavyweight paper for high-quality prints, such as posters or presentation materials. Lastly, papers exceeding 180 gsm are extra thick, commonly utilized for business cards or packaging materials.</p></p>
