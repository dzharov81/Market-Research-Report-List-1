<p><p>The gum base market encompasses various types such as SBR, Butyl Rubber, Polyisobutylene, Resins, Waxes, and Others. SBR (Styrene Butadiene Rubber) is a synthetic rubber widely used in chewing gum production, known for its high durability and flexibility. Butyl Rubber is another type of synthetic rubber known for its excellent impermeability to gases, used mainly in bubble gum production. Polyisobutylene is a polymer used as a chewing gum base, providing elasticity and adhesive properties. Resins, including polyvinyl acetate, contribute to the gum's texture and chewiness. Waxes, such as paraffin or beeswax, provide the gum its smoothness and shine. Lastly, Others refer to other materials used in gum base production, such as elastomers and fillers.</p></p>
