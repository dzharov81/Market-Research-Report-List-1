<p><p>The Decabromodiphenyl Ethane market is categorized into three types - the Bromine Method Preparation, Dichloroethane Solvent Method Preparation, and Others. The Dichloroethane Solvent Method Preparation utilizes dichloroethane as a solvent to facilitate the reaction between bromine and diphenyl ethane.
