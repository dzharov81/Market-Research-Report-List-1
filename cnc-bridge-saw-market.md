<p><p>The CNC bridge saw market consists of three types: 3 axis, 4 axis, and 5 axis. In a 3 axis CNC bridge saw, the cutting head moves along the x, y, and z axes, allowing for straight cuts in three directions. A 4 axis CNC bridge saw adds an additional rotating axis, enabling it to make angled cuts. The top-of-the-line 5 axis CNC bridge saw further enhances cutting precision by introducing a rotating and tilting axis, allowing for complex cuts at various angles. These different types cater to varying customer needs and offer increasing levels of cutting versatility and precision.</p></p>
