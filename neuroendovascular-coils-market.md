<p><p>Neuroendovascular coils are medical devices used in the treatment of intracranial aneurysms and other neurovascular diseases. The increasing prevalence of neurovascular diseases, such as brain aneurysms and arteriovenous malformations, is one of the key drivers of market growth. Additionally, the rising geriatric population, which is more susceptible to these conditions, further fuels the demand for neuroendovascular coils.</p><p>Moreover, technological advancements in coil design and the development of bioactive or flow-diverter coils are also contributing to the market's growth.
