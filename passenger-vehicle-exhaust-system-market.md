<p><p>A passenger vehicle exhaust system is a crucial component of the vehicle that helps to control and minimize emissions while also reducing noise. The catalytic converter helps to reduce harmful pollutants in the exhaust gases, while the muffler and tailpipe help to reduce noise and direct the exhaust gases out of the vehicle.</p><p>The future outlook of the passenger vehicle exhaust system market looks promising.
