<p><p>The Food and Beverage AGV market comprises three main types of AGVs: Unit Load Type, Automated Forklift Type, and Tugger Type. </p><p>The Unit Load Type AGVs are designed to transport large loads, such as pallets or containers, across the food and beverage facility. They are versatile and can navigate through narrow spaces efficiently. </p><p>Automated Forklift Type AGVs resemble conventional forklifts and are employed for lifting and stacking pallets. They eliminate the need for manual intervention in material handling tasks, improving efficiency and reducing human error.</p><p>Tugger Type AGVs, also known as tow tractors, are used to transport multiple and interconnected carts or trailers, which can carry various loads simultaneously. They provide a flexible and efficient solution for intra-facility transportation in food and beverage industries.</p></p>
