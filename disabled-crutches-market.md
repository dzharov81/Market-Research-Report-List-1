<p><strong>The Disabled Crutches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Forearm Crutch</li><li>Underarm Or Axilla Crutch</li><li>Platform Crutch</li><li>Leg Support Crutch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Disabled crutches market types can be categorized into four main types. Forearm crutches provide support to the user's forearm and hand and are often used by individuals with long-term disabilities. Underarm or axilla crutches, usually made with padded underarm supports, help distribute weight evenly through the upper body. Platform crutches offer more stability and feature a platform for the forearm to rest on. Leg support crutches, as the name suggests, provide additional support to the injured leg. These crutch types serve to improve mobility and offer various options for individuals with different needs.</p></p>
