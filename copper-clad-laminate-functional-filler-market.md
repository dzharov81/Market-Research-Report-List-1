<p><p>Copper clad laminate functional fillers are used to enhance the properties of copper clad laminates, which are widely employed in electronic circuit boards. Various types of fillers are available in the market, including silica fume, aluminum hydroxide, titania, aluminum nitride, hexagonal boron nitride, silicon carbide, and others. Silica fume is a fine powdery material that improves the conductivity and mechanical strength of laminates. Aluminum hydroxide enhances heat resistance, while titania improves flame retardancy. Aluminum nitride aids in thermal conductivity, hexagonal boron nitride improves insulation, and silicon carbide enhances mechanical properties. Other fillers may offer unique characteristics beneficial for specific applications.</p></p>
