<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Virtual dental consultation service market consists of two types: synchronous consultation service and asynchronous consultation service. Synchronous consultation service involves real-time communication between the dentist and the patient through video conferencing or live chat. It allows for immediate interaction and exchange of information. On the other hand, asynchronous consultation service involves the patient submitting their dental information, such as images or videos, to the dentist who reviews it offline and provides a response later. This type of service offers more flexibility in terms of timing and convenience for both the dentist and the patient.</p></p>
