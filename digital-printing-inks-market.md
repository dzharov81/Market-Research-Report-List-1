<p><p>Digital printing inks market consists of various types such as water-based, solvent-based, UV-cured, and others. Water-based inks are cost-effective, eco-friendly, and provide high-quality prints. Solvent-based inks offer durability and resistance to abrasion but may emit harmful VOCs. UV-cured inks dry instantly when exposed to UV light, making them ideal for high-speed printing with no smudging. Other types include sublimation inks, latex inks, and dye-based inks, each catering to specific printing requirements. Selection of ink type depends on factors like substrate compatibility, print durability, environmental impact, and print speed.</p></p>
