<p><p>Metallization pastes are used in the production of solar cells to create electrical contacts. The front side Ag paste is applied on the front surface of the solar cell to form a conductive grid, allowing the absorption of sunlight. The rear side Ag paste is applied on the back surface to provide a path for the current generated by the sunlight. The rear side Al paste, on the other hand, is an alternative to Ag paste and is used in some solar cell designs. It provides the necessary conductivity for the rear surface of the solar cell.</p></p>
