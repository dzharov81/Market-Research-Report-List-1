<p><ul><li>Liquid Oxygen System</li><li>Gaseous Oxygen System</li><li>On Board Oxygen Generation System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aerospace oxygen system market consists of three types of systems: liquid oxygen systems, gaseous oxygen systems, and on-board oxygen generation systems. </p><p>Liquid oxygen systems store and provide oxygen in a liquid form, which is converted into a gas when needed. Gaseous oxygen systems store and provide oxygen in a compressed gas form. On-board oxygen generation systems produce oxygen on-board the aircraft through chemical reactions or separation processes. These systems ensure the availability of oxygen for crew and passengers during high altitude flights and in case of emergencies.</p></p>
