<p><p>The piezoelectric diaphragm market offers three main types: external drive type, self-drive type, and other markets. The external drive type incorporates a separate energy source to drive the diaphragm, providing efficient and accurate sound generation. Self-drive type devices, on the other hand, use the energy generated by the diaphragm itself for operation, resulting in a simplified and cost-effective design. The other market segment encompasses various applications such as acoustic sensors, ultrasonic devices, and energy harvesting systems that utilize piezoelectric diaphragms.
