<p><strong>What are the Emerging Trends in the Global Smart and Connected Elevators market?</strong></p>
<p><p>The global smart and connected elevators market is witnessing several emerging trends. One of the key trends is the integration of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning capabilities in elevators, which allows for better predictive maintenance and improved energy efficiency.
