<p><p>Wireless Communication Technology for Automotive Market includes two types: DSRC and Mesh. DSRC (Dedicated Short Range Communication) enables vehicles to communicate with each other and with roadside infrastructure, enhancing transportation safety and efficiency. It works on a specific frequency band and has a range of around 300 meters. On the other hand, the Mesh market utilizes a network of interconnected devices, allowing vehicles to form a communication network. This enables them to share information and relay signals, creating an efficient and reliable communication system among vehicles.</p></p>
