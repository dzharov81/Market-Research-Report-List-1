<p><p>The Digital Control Valve Positioners market includes two main types: Single Acting Positioners and Double Acting Positioners. Single Acting Positioners are used in situations where the control valve needs to function in one direction only, either as an open or closed valve. On the other hand, Double Acting Positioners are designed for applications that require bi-directional control valve operation, allowing the valve to operate in both open and closed positions. These positioners play a crucial role in regulating valve movements, improving accuracy, and ensuring efficient operation in various industrial processes.</p></p>
