<p><p>Plastic trunking is a type of cable management system used to conceal and protect wires and cables in various settings. Two common types are mini trunking and maxi trunking. Mini trunking is smaller in size and typically used for lighter cable loads and limited spaces. It provides a neat and organized appearance while ensuring cables are safely hidden. On the other hand, maxi trunking is larger and designed for heavier cable loads and larger spaces. It offers more capacity and versatility for managing a greater number of cables efficiently.</p></p>
