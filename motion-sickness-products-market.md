<p><p>Motion sickness products are classified into two main categories: medicine and equipment. Medicine includes over-the-counter and prescription drugs that help alleviate symptoms of motion sickness such as nausea, dizziness, and vomiting. These medications work by either calming the vestibular system or blocking neurotransmitters responsible for motion sickness. On the other hand, equipment market refers to devices like motion sickness wristbands or patches that use acupressure or transdermal technology to stimulate specific pressure points or deliver medication through the skin, providing relief from motion sickness. Both types of products aim to address and manage motion sickness symptoms effectively.</p></p>
