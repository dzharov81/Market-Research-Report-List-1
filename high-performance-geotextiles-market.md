<p><p>High Performance Geotextiles are specialized materials used in construction and civil engineering projects to enhance soil stability, drainage, erosion control, and reinforcement. There are three main types: Non Woven, Woven, and Knitted. Non Woven Geotextiles are made from random fibers, providing excellent filtration and drainage properties. Woven Geotextiles are created by weaving polypropylene tapes, offering high strength and soil stabilization. Knitted Geotextiles are manufactured using synthetic yarns and offer good drainage and reinforcement properties. Each type serves different purposes based on their unique characteristics and applications in the construction industry.</p></p>
