<p><p>The Diesel Engine Catalyst market comprises several types, including DOC (Diesel Oxidation Catalyst), ASC (Ammonia Slip Catalyst), SCR (Selective Catalytic Reduction), POC (Particulate Oxidation Catalyst), and others. DOC aids in reducing harmful emissions by catalyzing the oxidation of exhaust gases. ASC helps prevent ammonia slip during the SCR process, reducing ammonia emissions. SCR uses a catalyst to convert harmful NOx into nitrogen and water. POC assists in oxidation and filtration of particulate matter. The "others" category includes various catalysts utilized for controlling specific pollutants in diesel engines.</p></p>
