<p><strong>The Aircraft Decorative Laminates Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Film Laminates</li><li>Reinforced Laminates</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aircraft decorative laminates market consists of two types: film laminates and reinforced laminates. Film laminates refer to thin layers of plastic or resin that are applied to aircraft surfaces for decorative purposes, providing a seamless and smooth finish. Reinforced laminates involve a combination of thin layers of plastic or resin with reinforcing materials such as glass fibers. These laminates provide additional strength and durability to aircraft surfaces, making them resistant to wear, tear, and physical damage. Both types of laminates offer aesthetic appeal, protection, and improved performance to aircraft interiors.</p></p>
