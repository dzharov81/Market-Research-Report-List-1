<p><strong>The Decorative Laminates Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High-pressure Laminate</li><li>Low-pressure Laminate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Decorative laminates are widely used in the construction and furniture industries. High-pressure laminate (HPL) is a durable and versatile material made by applying high pressure and temperature to multiple layers of resin-impregnated kraft paper. It offers excellent resistance to scratch, heat, impact, and moisture. Low-pressure laminate (LPL), on the other hand, is manufactured by pressing onto a substrate, offering a cost-effective option with lesser durability. Both HPL and LPL cater to various design preferences and applications, enhancing the aesthetic appearance of surfaces while providing protection and longevity.</p></p>
