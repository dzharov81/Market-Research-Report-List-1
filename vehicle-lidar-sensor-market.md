<p><p>A Vehicle Lidar Sensor is a remote sensing technology that uses laser light to create high-resolution maps of the surrounding environment. It stands for Light Detection and Ranging, and it works by emitting laser beams and measuring the time it takes for the light to bounce back after hitting objects in its path. This data is then used to create a detailed 3D representation of the surroundings, enabling the vehicle to detect and identify obstacles, pedestrians, and other vehicles on the road.</p><p>The Vehicle Lidar Sensor Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.5% during the forecast period.
