<p><p>Food grade magnesium oxide is a mineral compound used in food and beverage products. Light magnesium oxide is a fine and fluffy powder, while heavy magnesium oxide is denser and has larger particle sizes. Light magnesium oxide is preferred for its high reactivity and ability to act as a buffering agent in food and beverages. Heavy magnesium oxide, on the other hand, is commonly used as an anti-caking agent due to its lower reactivity. Both types are essential in maintaining quality and safety standards in the food industry.</p></p>
