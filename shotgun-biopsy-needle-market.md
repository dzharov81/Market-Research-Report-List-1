<p><p>A shotgun biopsy needle is a medical device used to obtain tissue samples from various organs for diagnostic purposes. It is called a "shotgun" needle because it has multiple side ports that allow for the collection of multiple tissue samples in a single pass. This minimizes the need for repeated insertions and reduces the risk of complications.</p><p>The global shotgun biopsy needle market is expected to witness substantial growth in the coming years. Factors contributing to this growth include the rising incidence of cancer, increasing demand for minimally invasive procedures, and advancements in biopsy needle technologies. Additionally, the growing geriatric population, which is more susceptible to various diseases, is also expected to drive market growth.</p><p>In terms of market analysis, the shotgun biopsy needle market is segmented based on type, application, end-user, and geography. The types of shotgun biopsy needles include reusable and disposable needles. The applications of these needles range from diagnosing cancer to obtaining tissue samples from organs like the liver, kidney, breast, and prostate.</p><p>On the basis of end-users, the market is categorized into hospitals, diagnostic centers, and research laboratories.
