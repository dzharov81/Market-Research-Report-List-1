<p><p>Volatile Corrosion Inhibitors (VCI) packaging is a type of protective packaging that is designed to prevent corrosion and rusting of metal components. These compounds form a thin layer on the surface of the metal, preventing the oxidation process that leads to corrosion. VCI packaging is available in various forms such as films, papers, and bags, which can be designed to wrap or enclose metal components of different sizes and shapes.</p><p>The future outlook for the VCI packaging market is positive, with strong growth expected in the coming years.
