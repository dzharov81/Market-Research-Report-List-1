<p><p>Direct acting electric electronic expansion valves are a type of valve that directly controls the flow of refrigerant in HVAC and refrigeration systems. They work by modulating the opening of the valve based on electronic signals, allowing for precise control of the refrigerant flow. On the other hand, decelerating type electric electronic expansion valves are designed to gradually open and close, reducing the impact of sudden changes in refrigerant flow. This type of valve helps to regulate the refrigerant flow smoothly, preventing abrupt changes in temperature and pressure in the system.</p></p>
