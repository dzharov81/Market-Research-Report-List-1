<p><strong>The Ketone Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ketone resins are classified into two types - Type I and Type II - based on their chemical properties and applications. Type I ketone resins are typically low molecular weight resins with excellent compatibility, adhesion, and flexibility. They are commonly used in applications such as lacquers, coatings, and printing inks. On the other hand, Type II ketone resins have higher molecular weight and are known for their superior heat resistance and chemical stability. They find extensive use in industries like automotive coatings, packaging, and adhesives.</p></p>
