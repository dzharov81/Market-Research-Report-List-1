<p><p>Silica aerogel insulation materials are categorized into three types: blanket, particle, and other markets. The blanket market includes silica aerogel materials that are typically in the form of rolls or sheets, which can be easily applied onto surfaces, such as walls, roofs, and pipes, to provide thermal insulation. The particle market comprises small particles of silica aerogel that are used for cavity wall insulation or as fillers in other insulation products. The other market category encompasses various forms of silica aerogel insulation, such as granules or panels, catering to specific insulation needs in industries like aerospace or automotive.</p></p>
