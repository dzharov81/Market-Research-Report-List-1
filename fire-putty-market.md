<p><p>The fire putty market consists of two main types: elastomeric and intumescent. Elastomeric fire putty is designed to expand and contract with temperature fluctuations, making it suitable for applications where flexibility is required. On the other hand, intumescent fire putty is formulated to swell when exposed to high temperatures, creating an insulating barrier that slows down the spread of fire. Both types of fire putty are used in various industries, such as construction and manufacturing, to enhance fire safety measures and protect against potential hazards.</p></p>
