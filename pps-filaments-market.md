<p><p>The PPS (Polyphenylene Sulfide) filament market encompasses two main types: multifilament and monofilament. Multifilament refers to a type of PPS filament that is composed of multiple small threads grouped together into a single strand. This type is known for its strength, flexibility, and durability, making it suitable for various applications. On the other hand, monofilament PPS filament consists of a single thread that offers distinct properties, such as high stiffness and excellent dimensional stability. Both multifilament and monofilament play vital roles in the PPS filament market, catering to different needs and requirements.</p></p>
