<p><p>The EVA (ethylene vinyl acetate) geomembrane market consists of three types: single layer geomembranes, double layer geomembranes, and three-layer geomembranes. Single layer geomembranes are made of a single sheet of EVA and are commonly used for basic applications. Double layer geomembranes are composed of two layers of EVA, enhancing their strength and durability, making them suitable for more demanding projects. Three-layer geomembranes, as the name suggests, comprise three layers of EVA and offer even greater performance in terms of strength and resistance to chemicals and UV radiation.</p></p>
