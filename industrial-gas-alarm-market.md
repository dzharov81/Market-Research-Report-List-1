<p><p>The Industrial Gas Alarm Market consists of two main types: Catalytic Type and Infrared Optical. The Catalytic Type gas alarm utilizes a catalytic sensor to detect flammable gases, functioning through a combustible gas concentration in the air. On the other hand, the Infrared Optical gas alarm uses an infrared sensor to detect the presence of gases based on their absorption of infrared light. Both types are integral for detecting and alerting individuals in industrial settings about the presence of dangerous gases, ensuring timely precautions and overall safety.</p></p>
