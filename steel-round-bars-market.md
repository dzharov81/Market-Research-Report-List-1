<p><ul><li>Hot-Rolled Steel Round Bars</li><li>Cold-Rolled Steel Round Bars</li><li>Forged Steel Round Bars</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steel round bars market consists of three main types: hot-rolled steel round bars, cold-rolled steel round bars, and forged steel round bars. Hot-rolled bars are produced by heating and rolling steel billets, resulting in a rough surface finish with improved mechanical properties. Cold-rolled bars are created through a process of cold drawing, resulting in a smoother surface finish and increased dimensional accuracy. Forged bars are made by heating steel billets and physically shaping them, leading to enhanced strength and durability. Each type caters to different applications and industry requirements.</p></p>
