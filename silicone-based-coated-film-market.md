<p><p>The silicone-based coated film market includes various types of films with different substrates. PE substrate siliconized film refers to a film where silicone is coated onto a PE (polyethylene) substrate. Similarly, PP substrate siliconized film involves the coating of silicone onto a PP (polypropylene) substrate. Lastly, the "Others" category includes silicone-based coated films with substrates other than PET, PE, or PP, such as PVC (polyvinyl chloride) or nylon.</p></p>
