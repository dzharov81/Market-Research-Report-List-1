<p><p>The automotive xenon lighting market consists of three different types: dipped headlights, high beams, and fog lamps. Dipped headlights are used for normal driving conditions and provide adequate illumination while not blinding other drivers. High beams are used to provide maximum visibility on dark roads and are typically used in areas with no streetlights. Fog lamps are designed specifically for use in foggy conditions as they emit a low and wide beam of light that is less likely to be reflected by the fog.</p></p>
