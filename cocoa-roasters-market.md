Nib drum roasters use a rotating drum to roast cocoa nibs, providing gentle and consistent heat distribution. Compact cylindrical roasters are designed for small-scale operations, offering portability and efficiency. Cylindrical batch roasters roast cocoa beans in small batches for better control over the roasting process. Other market types in cocoa roasters may include specialty or custom-designed roasters catering to unique industry needs.</p></p>
