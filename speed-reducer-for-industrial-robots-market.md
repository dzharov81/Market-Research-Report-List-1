<p><p>Speed reducers are essential components used in industrial robots to control the rotational speed and torque of the robot's joints or manipulators. The RV speed reducer type, also known as cycloidal gearboxes, features a toothed outer ring and eccentrics, providing high torque, compact size, and precision motion. Harmonic reducers employ a flexible spline and wave generator, resulting in high torque, precision, and backlash-free operation. The "Others" market segment includes various types of speed reducers, such as parallel shaft, planetary, and worm gear reducers, offering different performance characteristics to suit specific robot applications.</p></p>
