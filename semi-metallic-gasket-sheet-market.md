<p><p>The Semi-Metallic Gasket Sheet market includes two main types: Flat Flange and Non-planar Flange. A Flat Flange is a type of gasket sheet that is used between two flat surfaces to create a seal. It is commonly used in applications where there is minimal movement or vibration. On the other hand, a Non-planar Flange gasket sheet is used in situations where the flange surfaces are not flat or have irregular shapes. This type of gasket sheet is designed to accommodate the non-planar surface and provide a secure seal.</p></p>
