<p><p>Automatic screw oil press is a machine used for extracting oil from various types of oil seeds such as soybean, peanut, sunflower, rapeseed, sesame, and others. It is widely used in both small-scale and large-scale oil processing industries.</p><p>The global market for automatic screw oil press is projected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
