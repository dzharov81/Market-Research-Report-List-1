<p><strong>The Pedal Spot Welder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Point</li><li>Two-point</li><li>Multipoint</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Pedal spot welders are categorized into three market types based on the number of electrode points they possess. Single point welders have only one electrode, which applies pressure and heat to the metal parts being welded. Two-point welders have two electrodes that work simultaneously to join metal parts. Multipoint welders consist of multiple electrodes positioned strategically to weld different areas of an object simultaneously. These three market types cater to varying welding requirements, offering flexibility and efficiency in different applications.</p></p>
