<p><p>The Combined Heat and Power (CHP) System Market comprises four major types: gas and steam turbine, reciprocating engine, fuel cell, and microturbine. The gas and steam turbine market utilizes gas and steam turbines to generate electricity and capture waste heat for heating or cooling purposes. Reciprocating engine systems use internal combustion engines to produce electricity and utilize the waste heat. Fuel cell systems generate electricity through an electrochemical process, while capturing heat as a byproduct. Microturbine systems employ small gas turbines to generate electricity and recover waste heat for various applications.</p></p>
