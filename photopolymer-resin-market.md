<p><p>Photopolymer resin is a type of polymer that hardens when exposed to ultraviolet (UV) light. Photopolymer resin is commonly used in various applications, including 3D printing, flexographic printing plates, lithographic printing plates, and coatings.</p><p>The global photopolymer resin market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The market is driven by the increasing demand for 3D printing technology across several industries, such as automotive, aerospace, healthcare, and consumer goods. <p><p>The Photopolymer Resin Market encompasses various types such as Onium Salts, Organometallic, Pyridinium Salts, Oligomers and Monomers, and Other market segments. Organometallic compounds contain metal atoms coordinated with organic ligands, contributing to improved resin properties. Pyridinium salts act as a curing agent, providing increased stability to the resin. Oligomers and monomers are the fundamental building blocks of photopolymer resins, responsible for their reactivity.
