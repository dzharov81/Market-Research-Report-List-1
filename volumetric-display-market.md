<p><ul><li>Swept-Volume Display</li><li>Solid-Volume Display</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Volumetric displays are emerging technologies that depict 3D images in space without the need for additional visual aids. Swept-Volume Display, a type of volumetric display, projects images by rapidly moving a light source through space to create an illusion of a solid shape. Solid-Volume Display, on the other hand, builds the image by illuminating voxels (3D pixels) arranged within a physical medium. These displays offer a unique and immersive visual experience, with potential applications in medical imaging, gaming, and other fields requiring realistic 3D representations.</p></p>
