<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Jetting pumps are commonly categorized into three types: deep well, shallow well, and convertible. Deep well jetting pumps are designed specifically for wells with a depth greater than 25 feet, providing high-pressure performance to pump water from deep underground sources. Shallow well jetting pumps are used for wells with a depth of 25 feet or less, delivering a lower pressure for extracting water from relatively shallow sources. Convertible jetting pumps can be used for both deep and shallow wells, offering flexibility in pumping water from different depths.</p></p>
