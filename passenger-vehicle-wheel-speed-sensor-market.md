<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The passenger vehicle wheel speed sensor market comprises two types: Hall type and magnetic electric type. Hall type sensors utilize a Hall effect sensor that measures the magnetic field and detects the wheel speed. Magnetic electric type sensors, on the other hand, use a magnetic field to generate an electric signal as the wheel rotates, indicating the speed. These sensors play a crucial role in providing accurate data to vehicle control systems, ensuring efficient operation and safety on the roads.</p></p>
