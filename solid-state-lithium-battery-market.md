<p><p>The solid-state lithium battery market can be divided into two types: polymer-based solid-state lithium batteries and solid-state lithium batteries with inorganic solid electrolytes. Polymer-based solid-state lithium batteries use a polymer electrolyte, which offers flexibility and low cost, making them suitable for applications like wearable devices and portable electronics. Solid-state lithium batteries with inorganic solid electrolytes use ceramic materials as the electrolyte, providing high energy density and improved safety features, making them ideal for electric vehicles and grid storage systems. Overall, these battery technologies are advancing the field of energy storage and enabling various applications.</p></p>
