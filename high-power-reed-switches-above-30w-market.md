<p><p>High power reed switches refer to reed switches with a power capacity exceeding 30W. The market for these switches can be categorized into two types: Normally Open (NO) type and Normally Closed (NC) type. In the Normally Open type market, the switches are designed to have their contacts open in the absence of a magnetic field. In contrast, the Normally Closed type market involves switches with contacts closed by default, and they open only when a magnetic field is present. These types cater to diverse industrial applications that require high power handling capabilities in different magnetic field conditions.</p></p>
