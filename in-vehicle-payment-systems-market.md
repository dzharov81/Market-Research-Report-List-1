<p><strong>The In-Vehicle Payment Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Embedded System</li><li>Mooring System</li><li>Integrated System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The in-vehicle payment systems market consists of three types: the embedded system, mooring system, and integrated system. </p><p>1. Embedded system: This type refers to the built-in payment system within the vehicle, allowing drivers to complete transactions seamlessly without relying on external devices. </p><p>2. Mooring system: It involves connecting the vehicle to a payment infrastructure outside the vehicle, such as a mobile app or payment terminal, enabling users to make transactions from within the vehicle. </p><p>3. Integrated system: This type integrates the in-vehicle payment system with other vehicle functionalities, providing a seamless user experience and offering features like ordering fuel, food, or toll payments directly through the vehicle dashboard.</p></p>
