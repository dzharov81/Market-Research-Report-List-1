A self-propelled cleaner-loader is a standalone machine that is capable of both cleaning and loading materials, equipped with its own power source. Trailed cleaner-loaders are towed by another vehicle and require an external power source for operation. Semi-mounted cleaner-loaders are attached to the rear of a vehicle and are powered by the vehicle itself. These different market types offer varying levels of mobility and efficiency, catering to different needs and preferences in the cleaning and loading industry.</p></p>
