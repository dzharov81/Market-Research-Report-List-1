<p><p>Additive masterbatch is a type of masterbatch used in various industries to enhance the properties of plastic products. It can be classified into different types, including white masterbatch, black masterbatch, color masterbatch, and filler masterbatch. White masterbatch is used to achieve a uniform white color in plastic products, while black masterbatch is used for producing black-colored products. Color masterbatch is used to add vibrant colors to plastics. Additive masterbatch is used to impart specific properties like UV stability, antistatic nature, or flame retardancy to plastic products. Filler masterbatch is used to increase the weight and reduce the cost of plastic products by adding fillers like calcium carbonate.</p></p>
