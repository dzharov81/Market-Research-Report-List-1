<p><p>Respiratory Protective Equipment (RPE) refers to different types of equipment designed to protect individuals from inhaling harmful substances in the air. Non-Powered Air Respirators (NPAR) rely on the wearer's inhalation to draw air through filters, while Powered Air Respirators (PAR) use battery-powered fans to force air through filters. Self-Contained Breathing Apparatus (SCBA) provides the highest level of respiratory protection, supplying clean breathing air from compressed cylinders. These different types cater to varying levels of respiratory hazards and are used in industries such as construction, mining, and firefighting.</p></p>
