<p><p>An automotive cornering lamp, also known as a cornering light, is a safety feature in vehicles that enhances visibility while turning or navigating corners. These lamps are mounted on the front corners of the vehicle and provide additional illumination to the sides of the car during low-speed maneuvers or when the turn signals are activated. The purpose of cornering lamps is to improve visibility for the driver, reducing the chances of accidents caused by blind spots or poor lighting conditions.</p><p>Currently, the automotive cornering lamp market is witnessing steady growth. The increasing demand for advanced safety features in vehicles, stringent government regulations regarding vehicle safety, and the rising adoption of luxury vehicles are the major factors driving market growth.
