<p><p>The automotive adaptive emergency brake lights market comprises two types: gas brake lights and LED brake lights. Gas brake lights utilize gas discharge technology to emit light when the brake pedal is pressed, providing a consistent and efficient lighting solution. On the other hand, LED brake lights use Light Emitting Diodes to generate light, offering enhanced brightness, durability, and energy efficiency. Both types of brake lights ensure improved visibility for surrounding drivers, reducing the risk of collisions and enhancing overall safety on the roads.</p></p>
