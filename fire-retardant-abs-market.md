<p><p>The Fire Retardant ABS market is categorized into two main types: Non-halogen Type and Halogen Type. Non-halogen Type refers to fire retardant ABS materials that do not contain halogens, such as chlorine or bromine. These materials are considered more environmentally friendly and have low toxicity. Halogen Type, on the other hand, includes fire retardant ABS materials that contain halogens. While halogens provide excellent flame retardant properties, they can release toxic gases when exposed to fire, making them less environmentally friendly.</p></p>
