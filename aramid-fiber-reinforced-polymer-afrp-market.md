<p><p>Aramid Fiber Reinforced Polymer (AFRP) is a composite material that consists of aramid fibers embedded in a polymer matrix. It is widely used in various industries due to its high strength, lightweight, and excellent thermal stability. AFRP is available in two market types: thermoplastic and thermoset. Thermoplastic AFRP can be melted and re-molded multiple times without undergoing chemical change, while thermoset AFRP undergoes a chemical reaction during the curing process, resulting in irreversible bonding between the fibers and the polymer matrix. Both types offer unique properties and find applications based on specific requirements in different industries.</p></p>
