<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The vinyl extrusion equipment market can be categorized into three types: direct extrusion, indirect extrusion, and hydrostatic extrusion. </p><p>Direct extrusion involves pushing the vinyl material through a die in a single continuous process. Indirect extrusion, on the other hand, involves the use of a container to hold the material while a ram pushes it through the die. </p><p>Hydrostatic extrusion entails the use of a pressurized fluid to facilitate the extrusion process. Each of these methods offers distinct advantages and is commonly used in the production of vinyl profiles, pipes, and other products.</p></p>
