High purity hexafluoroethane is commonly utilized for cleaning and etching silicon dioxide, silicon nitride, and metal films. Lastly, high purity carbon dioxide is employed for applications such as photoresist stripping and cleaning. These gases play a crucial role in ensuring the precision and efficiency of semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
