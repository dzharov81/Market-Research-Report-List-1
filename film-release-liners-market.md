Polyolefin liners are made from a type of thermoplastic polymer and are known for their excellent heat resistance and low friction properties. BO-PET liners are created by stretching a polyester film, resulting in high tensile strength and resistance to tearing. BOPP liners are made from a thermoplastic polymer with high clarity and good tensile strength. All these liners are used in various industries for applications such as adhesive labeling and graphic films.</p></p>
