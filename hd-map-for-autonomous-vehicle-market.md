<p><p>The Cloud-Based HD Map market for autonomous vehicles involves storing and accessing high-definition maps on cloud platforms, enabling real-time updates and sharing of data across various vehicles. It allows for advanced features like crowd-sourcing, real-time traffic data, and over-the-air updates. On the other hand, the Embedded HD Map market involves pre-installing the high-definition maps directly into the autonomous vehicles. This enables quick and offline access to the maps without relying on cloud connectivity, making it suitable for areas with limited or no internet connectivity. Both types of markets cater to the specific needs of autonomous vehicles regarding navigation and safety.</p></p>
