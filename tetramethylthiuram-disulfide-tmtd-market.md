<p><p>Tetramethylthiuram Disulfide (TMTD) is a chemical compound primarily used as an accelerator in rubber industry. It is available in three different market types: powder, spray-dried oil powder, and masterbatch. Powder form consists of finely ground particles, while spray-dried oil powder is a free-flowing blend of TMTD and oil. Masterbatch is a concentrated mixture of TMTD with a carrier resin, facilitating its easy incorporation into rubber compounds. These different market types provide options for manufacturers to choose the most suitable form for their specific rubber processing needs.</p></p>
