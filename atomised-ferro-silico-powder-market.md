<p><p>Atomised Ferro Silico Powder is a type of powder that is widely used in various industries. It is manufactured through different atomisation processes, namely water atomisation and gas atomisation. Water atomised powder is produced by forcing molten Ferro Silico through a high-pressure water jet, resulting in fine particles. On the other hand, gas atomised powder is created by spraying molten Ferro Silico into a high-velocity gas stream, also leading to the formation of small particles. Both types of atomisation methods have their advantages and applications in different industries, depending on the specific requirements.</p></p>
