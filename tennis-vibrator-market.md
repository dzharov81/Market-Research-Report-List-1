<p><ul><li>Granular</li><li>Ribbon</li><li>Cuboid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tennis vibrator market consists of three main types: granular, ribbon, and cuboid. Granular vibrators are designed to provide consistent and even vibrations throughout the tennis racket. Ribbon vibrators, on the other hand, have a slim and elongated shape, allowing for more focused vibrations along specific areas of the racket. Lastly, cuboid vibrators offer a combination of both granular and ribbon features, providing versatility in vibration patterns and intensities. These different market types cater to various preferences and playing styles of tennis players, aiming to enhance their performance on the court.</p></p>
