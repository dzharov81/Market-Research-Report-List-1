<p><p>Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) membranes are widely used in various industries due to their exceptional properties. There are two main types; plasticized PVC membranes and unplasticized PVC membranes. Plasticized PVC membranes contain plasticizers, which make them flexible and more suitable for applications that require ease of bending and shaping. On the other hand, unplasticized PVC membranes are rigid and lack plasticizers, making them more resistant to chemicals and extreme temperatures. Both types have their own advantages, and choosing the right one depends on the specific requirements of the intended application.</p></p>
