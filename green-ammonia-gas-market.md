<p><p>The green ammonia gas market can be categorized into three types: alkaline water electrolysis, proton exchange membrane, and solid oxide electrolysis. Alkaline water electrolysis involves the use of an alkaline solution as an electrolyte to produce ammonia gas. Proton exchange membrane technology employs a proton-conductive membrane to facilitate the separation of hydrogen and nitrogen gases during the production process. Solid oxide electrolysis uses a solid oxide electrolyte to generate ammonia gas. These different types offer varying efficiency, cost, and scalability for the production of green ammonia gas.</p></p>
