<p><ul><li>Chemical Additive</li><li>Mineral Additive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The concrete additive market is divided into two main types: chemical additives and mineral additives. Chemical additives are substances that are added to concrete to enhance its properties, such as strength, durability, and workability. They include admixtures like water reducers, accelerators, and air-entraining agents. On the other hand, mineral additives are natural or synthetic materials that are used as partial replacements for cement in concrete mixtures. These additives, such as fly ash and silica fume, can improve the performance and sustainability of concrete while reducing its carbon footprint.</p></p>
