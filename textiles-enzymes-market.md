<p><p>Textile enzymes are substances used in the textile industry to enhance various processes. There are different types of enzymes used, including cellulase, amylase, catalase, pectinase, laccase, and others. Cellulase enzymes help in the breakdown of cellulose fibers, amylase enzymes assist in the removal of starch-based impurities, catalase enzymes aid in the removal of hydrogen peroxide, pectinase enzymes help in degrading pectin, laccase enzymes are used for bleaching purposes, and other enzymes serve specific textile-related functions. These enzymes play a crucial role in improving textile quality, efficiency, and sustainability.</p></p>
