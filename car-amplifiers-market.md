<p><p>Car amplifiers are electronic devices that enhance the audio quality in a car's audio system. On the other hand, Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM) car amplifiers are pre-installed by car manufacturers during production. These amplifiers are specifically designed for a particular car model and are integrated into the car's audio system. OEM amplifiers offer convenience, as they seamlessly fit into the car's interior design and are factory-tested for compatibility.</p></p>
