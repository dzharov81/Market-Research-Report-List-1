<p><p>Natural film forming polymers are used in various industries such as cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and food. Water-soluble film forming polymers are those that dissolve in water and create a thin film when dried, providing excellent adhesion and flexibility. These polymers are commonly used in skincare and haircare products. On the other hand, oil-soluble film forming polymers do not dissolve in water but are soluble in oils. They are primarily used in products such as lipsticks, sunscreens, and foundations, where oil-based formulations are preferred.</p></p>
