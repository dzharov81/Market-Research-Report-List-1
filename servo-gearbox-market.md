<p><p>Servo Gearboxes are mechanical devices used to transmit motion and power from a servo motor to an output device. They come in different types based on their configuration. </p><p>Inline servo gearboxes have a parallel configuration, with the input and output shafts aligned in a straight line. They are suitable for applications requiring direct power transmission.</p><p>Right-angle servo gearboxes have a 90-degree configuration, where the input and output shafts are perpendicular to each other. They are ideal for applications with space constraints or when a change in direction is needed.</p><p>Other types of servo gearboxes may include planetary, worm, or bevel gear configurations, designed for diverse requirements such as high precision, high torque, or compact size.</p></p>
