<p><p>The Medical Grade Chitosan market comprises two main types: Chitosan HCl and Carboxymethyl Chitosan. Chitosan HCl is a form of chitosan that is obtained by dissolving chitosan in hydrochloric acid, resulting in a fine powder. It is commonly used in pharmaceuticals and medical devices due to its biocompatibility and antimicrobial properties. Carboxymethyl Chitosan, on the other hand, is obtained by chemically modifying chitosan with carboxymethyl groups. This type of chitosan is known for its ability to enhance drug delivery systems and its wound healing properties. Both types play a crucial role in medical applications, offering unique benefits for various healthcare purposes.</p></p>
