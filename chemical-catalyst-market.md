<p><p>Chemical catalysts, also known as catalysts or catalytic agents, are substances that increase the rate of a chemical reaction by providing an alternative pathway with lower activation energy. Catalysts play a vital role in the production of biofuels, which are considered as a greener alternative to fossil fuels. Moreover, the increasing focus on reducing greenhouse gas emissions has led to the development of catalysts that facilitate more efficient and eco-friendly chemical reactions.</p><p>In recent years, there has been a trend towards the development of heterogeneous catalysts, which are more efficient and versatile compared to traditional homogeneous catalysts. Moreover, rapid industrialization and urbanization in the region have further fuelled the demand for chemical catalysts.</p><p>Overall, the chemical catalyst market is expected to witness steady growth in the coming years, driven by the increasing demand for sustainable energy sources and the development of more efficient catalysts.</p></p>
