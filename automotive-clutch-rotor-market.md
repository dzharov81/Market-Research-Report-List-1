<p><p>The automotive clutch rotor market consists of three types: 1 Piece Rotor Double Flux Type, 1 Piece Rotor Triple Flux Type, and 2 Piece Rotor Double Flux Type. The 1 Piece Rotor Double Flux Type refers to a clutch rotor design that has a single piece with two flux paths. The 1 Piece Rotor Triple Flux Type, on the other hand, has a single piece but with three flux paths. Lastly, the 2 Piece Rotor Double Flux Type has two separate pieces and two flux paths. These different types cater to varying performance and efficiency requirements in automotive clutch systems.</p></p>
