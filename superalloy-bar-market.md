<p><p>The superalloy bar market is categorized into different types based on the primary elements used to make these alloys. Iron-based superalloy bars are composed mainly of iron and contain small amounts of other elements like chromium, nickel, and cobalt. Nickel-based superalloy bars are primarily composed of nickel and have additional elements like chromium, iron, and cobalt. Cobalt-based superalloy bars are predominantly made of cobalt and contain other elements like chromium, nickel, and tungsten. Lastly, the "Others" category includes superalloy bars that are composed of different combinations of elements not classified under iron-based, nickel-based, or cobalt-based alloys.</p></p>
