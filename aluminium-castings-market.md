<p><ul><li>Die Casting</li><li>Permanent Mold Casting</li><li>Sand Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aluminium Castings Market includes three main types of casting processes: Die Casting, Permanent Mold Casting, and Sand Casting. Die Casting involves injecting molten aluminium into a mold under high pressure, resulting in complex shapes with high dimensional accuracy. Permanent Mold Casting uses reusable molds made of cast iron or steel to produce aluminium parts or products. In contrast, Sand Casting involves pouring molten aluminium into a sand mold, allowing for the creation of large and intricately shaped castings. Each casting method offers unique benefits, catering to different industry needs and specifications.</p></p>
