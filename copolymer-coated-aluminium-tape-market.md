<p><p>Copolymer coated aluminium tape is available in two types: single-sided and double-sided. Single-sided tape has the copolymer coating on only one side of the aluminium substrate. This type of tape is commonly used for various applications such as sealing HVAC systems, repairing damaged insulation, and electrical shielding. On the other hand, double-sided tape features the copolymer coating on both sides. It is often used for bonding applications where a strong and durable bond is required, such as joining panels or securing objects together.</p></p>
