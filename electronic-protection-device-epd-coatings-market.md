<p><p>The Electronic Protection Device (EPD) coatings market includes different types of application methods such as brushing, dipping, manual spray, and automatic spray. Brushing involves applying the coating using a brush, offering precise control but can be time-consuming for large-scale applications. Dipping entails immersing the electronic device into a bath of coating material, ensuring uniform coverage but might result in excess material. Manual spray involves manually spraying the coating onto the device, providing flexibility but may lead to uneven distribution. Automatic spray employs automated equipment for coating application, guaranteeing consistency and efficiency.</p></p>
